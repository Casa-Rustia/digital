FAQ

Questions clients usually ask before starting

Every business is different, so we begin with a short project review before confirming the best direction.

How do we start?

Send your inquiry through the contact page. We review your business, goals, pages needed, and preferred timeline, then recommend the best next step.

Can you work with international clients?

Yes. The website is planned for businesses that want professional digital services for local or international audiences.

Do I need content and photos ready?

Not always. We can help structure your service descriptions, page copy, visual direction, and required content checklist.

Can you build more than a website?

Yes. We can support CRM planning, forms, dashboards, automation, booking flows, and full-stack systems depending on your business needs.

How long does a project take?

Starter websites usually take 3 to 7 days, business websites usually take 1 to 3 weeks, and custom systems usually take 3 to 8 weeks. The timeline depends on content readiness, number of pages, features, feedback speed, and integrations.

How much does a project cost?

Starter websites begin at $300 USD, business websites begin at $650 USD, and custom systems begin at $1,500 USD. Services such as web design, CRM setup, automation, content support, and website care have their own starting prices shown on each page.
